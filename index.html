Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of our mobile application (hereinafter referred to as the "Application"). By downloading and using the Application, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Data Collection and Local Storage

Our Application is designed to operate completely offline and store data locally. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location to external servers. Your in-game progress, high scores, earned currency, and unlocked skins are stored entirely on your device's local storage.

2. Third-Party Services and Advertising Networks

The Application utilizes third-party advertising networks to provide in-game rewards (such as skins and revive mechanics). To facilitate this, the Application integrates the Google AdMob (Google Ads) SDK.

Google AdMob may collect and process non-personal data, including your device's unique advertising identifier (Advertising ID), device model, operating system version, and general analytical data, to serve relevant advertisements and measure ad performance. The collection and processing of this data are strictly governed by Google's own privacy policy.

3. Children's Privacy

Our Application is intended for a general audience (PEGI 3). The advertisements displayed through our ad network integrations are filtered to ensure they comply with child safety guidelines. Sensitive, objectionable, and age-inappropriate ad categories have been completely blocked via our Google AdMob management console.

4. User Rights and Contact Information

Since our Application does not collect any personal data, you can completely erase your locally stored in-game data (skins, progression, etc.) at any time by clearing the application data through your device settings or by uninstalling the Application.

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us via the developer contact address provided on the Google Play Console.
